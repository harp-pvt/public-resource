3. How User Data Is Stored

All data entered by the user is stored locally on the user's device using AsyncStorage, which is part of the device's sandboxed app storage. Specifically:

  • Data is not transmitted to any external server or cloud service
  • Data is not backed up to developer-operated servers
  • Data is not accessible to other apps on the device
  • Data is not shared with any third party under any circumstances

Data persists on the device until the user explicitly deletes it using the in-app "Reset All Data" feature, or uninstalls the App.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Since all data resides on your device:

  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, device encryption)
  • No network transmission means no risk of interception during transfer
  • No server storage means no risk of server-side data breaches

We recommend users enable device-level security features (PIN, fingerprint, face unlock) and keep their operating system up to date.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the App is installed. You have full control over your data:

  • View: All your data is visible within the App at all times
  • Export: You can export your data as JSON from Settings → Export Data
  • Delete specific data: Use "Clear All Transactions" in Settings to remove expense records while keeping your setup
  • Delete all data: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings to permanently erase all data and return to the initial setup
  • Complete removal: Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your device
