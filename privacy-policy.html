Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

100% Offline • Zero Data Collection

TL;DR: Money Badger does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal or financial data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

1. Overview

Money Badger ("the App") is a personal finance management application developed by Sam ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

The App is designed to be completely offline. It does not connect to any servers, APIs, or cloud services.

2. Data We Collect

None.

Money Badger does not collect any data whatsoever. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial data (income, expenses, loans, savings)
  • Device information (model, OS version, identifiers)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

3. Data Storage

All data you enter into Money Badger (income, expenses, loans, savings, budget categories) is stored locally on your device only, using the device's built-in storage (AsyncStorage).

  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is stored in the cloud
  • No data is backed up to our servers
  • No data is shared with any third party

If you uninstall the App or use the "Reset All Data" feature, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

Money Badger does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No crash reporting (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media SDKs
  • No payment processors

5. Permissions

Money Badger requests only the following permissions:

  • Internet (Android) — Required by React Native framework for development builds. The App does not make any network requests in production.

The App does not request access to:

  • Camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Location services
  • Phone state or call logs
  • SMS or notifications (beyond local notifications)
  • External storage (beyond app-specific storage)

6. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures (screen lock, encryption, biometric authentication). We recommend:

  • Using a device lock (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face unlock)
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals

7. Children's Privacy

Money Badger is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the App collects no data at all, this is inherently satisfied.

8. Data Export & Deletion

  • Export: You can export your data as JSON from within the App (Settings → Export Data)
  • Delete: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings to permanently erase all data, or simply uninstall the App

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since we collect no data, changes will be minimal.

10. Open Source

Money Badger's source code is available for review. You can verify that no data collection occurs by inspecting the code yourself.
